Movement Animset Pro Lite v.1.2
--------------------


This is a demo of Movement Animset Pro - a complete pack of motion capture animations, to build a seamless third person perspective character movement for your game. The animations are universal, so you can use them for any setting you like - SciFi, Fantasy etc.


It consists of 8 animations:


Idle
WalkFwdLoop
WalkFwdStart
WalkFwdStop_LU
WalkFwdStop_RU
JumpIdleStart
JumpIdleLand
ButtonPush_RH
